Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Need a fluffy pancake recipe?

This one is just right, especially when there's plenty of butter and syrup. Make it extra special with berries and cream!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoon butter, melted
