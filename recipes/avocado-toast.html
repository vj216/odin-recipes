Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado toast is a deliciously hearty breakfast.

If you are not vegan, you can serve fried eggs on top for a delicious vegetarian meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 avocado, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Steps

  1. Toast bread in a toaster or toaster oven.

  2. Mash avocado, parsley, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder together in bowl.

  3. Spread on top of the toast slices.
