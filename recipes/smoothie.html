Mango-Peach Smoothie

Description

You can use fresh or frozen fruit in this yummy mango and peach smoothie.

It tastes better if the fruit is frozen, so I freeze fresh fruit for this.

Ingredients

  • 1 peach, sliced
  • 1 mango, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup vanilla soy milk
  • 1/2 cup orange juice, or as needed

Steps

  1. Put peach, mango, soy milk and orange juice into a blender.

  2. Cover, and puree until smooth.

  3. Pour into a glass to serve.

  4. Top it up with pieces of peach for visual appeal. Serve chilled.
